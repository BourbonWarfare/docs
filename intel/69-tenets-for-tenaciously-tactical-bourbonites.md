# 69 Tenets for Tenaciously Tactical Bourbonites

* A Sergeant in motion outranks a Lieutenant who doesn't know what's going on. - kilo
* Always slot Bradleys with all 3 crewmembers. The commander is essential for visibility. - kilo
* Boxloader crates are glitchy. Never trust a ViV-box, on a ViV-crate, on a ViV-vehicle. Max it out at 2 levels: Crate on a vic. Unless using pallets that allow for "packing" cargo. - kilo
* Never trust an arty computer. Always fire a spotting smoke round first. - Quiz
* Always rebind your countermeasures key so it's not close to eject. - Jdubs
* In CQB, if you are not down or immobile, keep moving until the building or compound is clear! Do not stop! - Athena
* Rebind your grenade throw to anything except G. - Classic
* A grenadier is an autorifleman's best friend. - Athena
* As a rifleman or other core role, always anticipate contact. Move along cover and concealment and figure out potential firing positions in case you suddenly get into a firefight. - Athena
* When SL/Pilot/Driver says mount, mount! Wait for Driver/Pilot to call dismount, then dismount! You don't want someone to call dismount while you are still in the air. - Dark Demise
* The pilots decision is final. A pilot should never do anything that endangers the aircraft or the passengers. - tcvm
* Don't backseat drive a vehicle that has a crew. - JJJustin
* 1 gun up and firing is better than 2 hands doing medical. - Woeful
* Never trust a vehicle that looks like it's done cooking off. It isn't. - Quiz
* Backblast hurts less than .50 cal. Just shoot the tank/IFV/helicopter - call it, and if they don't get away from your back it's their problem. - Athena
* If you stand behind someone holding a launcher, the RAT cannot be held responsible for what happens to you. - Athena
* Vote on recruit threads. - Athena
* If something goes wrong, blame Kilo. - Zohli
* Keep radio comms ABC: Accurate, BRIEF, and clear. - JJJustin
* Committing with full effort to a half baked plan is often better than committing with half effort to a full baked plan. - tcvm
* When under fire, do your job. If you weren't assigned one, ask for one or go find one. - Quiz
* "Roger" means "I heard you"; "Affirm" means "Yes". - Emma
* If you have e-tools and time, dig. If you don’t have time to dig, make time. - Woeful
* If the current approach or attack dynamic isn't working, there is no shame in pulling back to regroup and reassess. - Zohli
* Fire without maneuver is a waste of ammo, maneuver without fire is suicide. - tcvm
* PTT stands for Push To Talk, not Push To Think. Know what you're going to transmit before you start transmitting it - Quiz
* Grenades roll down hill. - tcvm
* Don’t be afraid to speak up if you think a plan is poor, but don’t backseat lead and insist a different plan would be better. - tcvm
* Don't clear a building or swing a corner solo. Phone a friend and mark your location. - Quiz
* After clearing a building, put a dot on the map. You can slow down the mission pace by not doing this! - tcvm
* Know your ammo counts. Nothing sucks more than fighting for your life and hearing click. - Aescula
* Range your rifle to 200, 300, or 400, and just aim center of mass. - Stringbones
* Never skyline or sit on the highest possible feature. Move down the face of the hill, or on a smaller hill slightly to the side. You're less likely to be seen. - Shade
* When closing with the enemy, remember SAM-K: Suppress, Assess, Move, Kill - Fen
* If you ND, simply shout "contact left" and watch the whole squad open up. No one can prove you _didn't_ hear something. - Fen (joke)
* SHOOT DA GUN - Athena
* The most effective CQB technique in the world is named "hucking a frag into the room". - Yeddi
* The door frame is not your friend. Get through it or past it as quickly as possible. - Yeddi
* If you're close enough to whisper to each other, you're too close together. - Yeddi
* If it's bigger than you, it has right of way. - Yeddi
* Armoured vics are not reliable cover! Their first instinct when taking heavy fire is often to move. - Athena
* If you have to check the map to figure out where your fireteam is, you've wandered too far away. - Yeddi
* When marking any enemy contact, also consider marking the movement of the contact with an arrow. - Hussar
* If you see a corpse/someone goes down, don’t approach until you’ve secured the immediate area and angles around the area - you might be walking into a kill zone! - Antares
* Smoke is more than just concealment for your team. Smoke the enemy positions to facilitate an assault, and smoke between yourself and the enemy to facilitate maneuver by breaking line of sight. You can also see enemies at the edge of smoke's concealment before they can see you. - Antares
* As a rule of thumb, the best possible points for a base of fire and best paths for maneuver are always being watched. - Antares
* Bullets penetrate - you don't need to breach the room if you can shoot through all the drywall next to the door first. - Antares
* Even with Babel on, screaming at the enemy at key moments when assaulting in CQB can effect their reaction time with you. Plus, it's funny to watch. - Antares
* Lead, follow, or get out of the way! - Athena
* An imperfect plan executed now is better than a perfect plan executed late. - Athena, Stringbones, Shade
